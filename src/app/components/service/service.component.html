Services

Visha consultants has been performing numerous Geotechnical Engineering Consulting services throughout the Bay Area and the Sacramento region from individual home owners to multi million dollar businesses. Following are the key geotechnical engineering services we provide:

  • Geotechnical Investigation (Soils) Report
  • Conventional and Segmental Retaining Wall Design
  • Foundation Design with Bearing Capacity Values
  • Pavement Design (Concrete and Asphalt Pavements)
  • Geotechnical Feasibility Studies & Site Investigations
  • Slope Stability Analysis & Landslide Study
  • Earthquake Analyses with Liquefaction Study
  • Develop Site Specific Seismic Ground Motion Parameters
  • Installing Groundwater Monitoring Wells and Percolation Tests
  • Building Settlement Evaluation and Inspection
  • Provide Field Inspection and Testing Services during Earthwork Fill Placement & Compaction.

We also perform field inspection and testing services during earthwork fill placement and compaction (grading), testing of street subgrade, aggregate base, and asphalt concrete, observation and testing of soil fill placement and compaction.
